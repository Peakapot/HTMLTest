Example Career Paths

Beginner

  • Start in SOC analyst, IT support, or junior GRC roles.
  • Build security fundamentals: networking, IAM, and incident response.
  • Learn safe AI usage and prompt risk controls for business teams.

Intermediate

  • Move into threat hunting, cloud security, or detection engineering.
  • Own AI model/data risk assessments and third-party AI reviews.
  • Lead policy implementation for secure AI adoption.

Experienced

  • Progress to security architect, AI security lead, or vCISO roles.
  • Design enterprise AI governance controls and red-team exercises.
  • Coach teams, report to leadership, and shape security strategy.

Industry Qualifications to Target

Core Security Credentials

  • CompTIA Security+
  • SC-900 / AZ-500 (cloud + identity security basics)
  • CISSP or CISM for leadership-track professionals

AI + Governance Credentials

  • ISO/IEC 42001 awareness and implementation training
  • NIST AI Risk Management Framework practical training
  • Vendor certifications in responsible AI security controls
